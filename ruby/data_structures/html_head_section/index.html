Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon TJ's Olive Oil
  • 1/2 package TJ's Portobello Mushrooms, diced
  • 1 package TJ's Beef-less Ground Beef
  • 1/2 cup plus 2 tablespoons TJ's Organic Spicy Taco Sauce
  • Your favorite TJ's Tortillas
  • 1/2 head of TJ's Iceberg Lettuce, shredded
  • 2 TJ's Roma Tomatoes, diced
  • TJ's Mild Shredded Cheddar Cheese

Beef-less Burritos from Trader Joe's

Description

Making a taco better than its already-delicious self sounds like a tall task, but then you probably haven’t tried a taco that’s been doused in Trader Joe’s Organic Spicy Taco Sauce. We start with a base of distilled vinegar and tomato paste; add some heat thanks to chili pepper powder, red jalapeño pepper purée, and cayenne peppers; and round out the flavor with spices like garlic powder, pepper, and oregano.

This savory, tangy, and spicy sauce isn’t so spicy that you’d have any qualms about dousing your food in it. And just because it’s called Taco Sauce doesn’t mean you have to use this stuff exclusively on tacos. Burritos, taquitos, tamales, quesadillas, nachos, scrambled eggs, burgers, chicken tenders... they’re are all fair game! In this recipe, we’ve simmered Trader Joe’s Beef-less Ground Beef and diced mushrooms in our Spicy Taco Sauce for a multipurpose, meatless taco or burrito filling with just the right amount of heat.

Directions

  1. Heat oil in a skillet over medium heat.
  2. Add diced mushrooms to skillet and let cook undisturbed for 5 minutes, until most of the moisture has evaporated.
  3. Add beef-less beef and taco sauce to skillet and sauté another 5–7 minutes, until heated through.
  4. To serve, scoop beef-less beef into a warm tortilla and top with lettuce, tomato, and cheese.
  5. Fold as you like and serve with more taco sauce for drizzling.
